Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI diagnostic tool?
Our model is trained on over 100,000 verified pathological images and currently operates at a 98.5% confidence rate for the 50 most common crop diseases. However, it should be used to assist, not replace, professional agronomist advice.
What file types are supported?
We currently support standard image formats including JPEG, PNG, and HEIC up to 10MB in size. For best results, ensure the leaf is well-lit and in sharp focus.
Is my data saved?
Images processed through the free scanner are not permanently stored unless you create a registered account. Diagnostic metadata is anonymized and used exclusively to improve the AI model.
